Neighbor embedding algorithms

This package aims to implement most popular neighbor embedding (NE) algorithms. In this section we briefly go through the main NE algorithms and their variants.

We refer to “A Probabilistic Graph Coupling View of Dimension Reduction” for a probabilistic point of view on these losses.

NE methods can be divided into two main categories: cross-entropy (CE) and binary cross-entropy (BCE) based methods.

Part I : cross entropy based objectives (t-SNE like)
